Hello !

I am Loïc Allègre, currently a PhD Student under the supervision of Christian Retoré, in the TEXTE team at LIRMM & University of Montpellier.

My research interests are centered around mathematical logic, mainly proof theory and its links to theoretical computer science.

More specifically, my work focuses on extensions and variants of quantification in various logical systems and formalisms, notably (but not limited to) Hilbert's Epsilon Calculus and generalized quantifiers.

I am also a firm believer that Deep Inference is very cool.
